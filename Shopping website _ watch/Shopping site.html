Braun Classic Watch

The Braun watch is a reissue of the original 1970's design from renowed design team Dietrich Lubs and Dieter Rams, both of which have work featured in the museum's collection. The large watch features a number face, and the smaller watch has only index lines. Made of a matte stainless-steel case, black dial.
